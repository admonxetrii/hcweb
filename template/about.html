About Hanuman Construction

Shri Salik Ram Neupane, the Proprietor and visionary, is a hard-working and dedicated professional having around 25 Yrs Experience in Civil related construction works. He has worked in both India and Nepal during the entire tenure of his professional career with reputed and prestigious Companies of India and Nepal.

He Joined Bridge & Roof Co. (India) Ltd., A Govt. of India Enterprise under Bharat Yantra Nigam Ltd. as Site Coordinator. In no time, he rose to fame due to his dedication to the works allotted.

As time passed by, he became a petty Contractor and started doing small construction works in many prestigious Plants like Cement, Steel, Aluminum & Oil Refinery Plants with awards for various types of buildings and important structures.
